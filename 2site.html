RELATIONAL DESIGN 32

About/

relational design is the creation by designers of
platforms or systems that empower users
to generate their work, effectively
becoming designers.

Features/


Values/

autonomous, independent, isolated,
and closed, relational design is synonymous with interdependence,
connectedness, and openness. It evokes today’s networkculture,
both literally and metaphorically, and the web of associations,
uses, and contexts determining design today.
